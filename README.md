# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Task 2

### Project Overview

This project focuses on Customer Retention and Churn Analysis using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The objective is to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and generate actionable business insights that can help improve customer retention.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Power BI as part of the Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship Program.

---

## Task Description

Customer churn is one of the most important metrics for subscription-based businesses because it directly impacts revenue and long-term growth.

The goal of this project was to:

* Analyze customer churn behavior
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Understand retention trends
* Generate business recommendations to reduce churn
* Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard

---

## Objectives

* Measure customer churn and retention rates
* Analyze churn across contract types
* Evaluate churn by internet service category
* Visualize customer retention metrics
* Generate business-focused recommendations

## Key Findings

### 1. Customer Churn Rate

The analysis revealed an overall churn rate of approximately 26.54%, indicating that more than one-quarter of customers discontinued the service.

### 2. Contract Type Impact

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts showed significantly higher churn compared to customers with One-Year and Two-Year contracts.

### 3. Internet Service Analysis

Fiber Optic customers exhibited the highest churn levels among all internet service categories.

### 4. Retention Insights

Customers with long-term contracts demonstrated stronger retention behavior and lower churn risk.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Encourage Long-Term Contracts

Provide discounts and loyalty benefits to encourage customers to switch from Month-to-Month contracts to annual plans.

### Improve Fiber Optic Customer Experience

Enhance service quality, customer support, and customer engagement initiatives for Fiber Optic users.

### Implement Retention Campaigns

Identify high-risk customer groups and develop targeted retention strategies.

### Monitor Churn Trends

Use business intelligence dashboards to continuously monitor customer behavior and retention performance.
